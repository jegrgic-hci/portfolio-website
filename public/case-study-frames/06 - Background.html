01 Background
Outcome
+0.63% incremental increase in Shops Ads GMV in H1’23
16% Net churn
Context
Within SMA, there is no method for sellers to enable and config Seller Funded Incentives (SFI) due to contractual and technical blockers of email capture between Shopify and SMA. However, as of February 2023, an agreement has been reached to allow the set-up and config of SFI email capture within SMA. This new feature is expected increase seller adoption of incentives, improving Meta's price parity situation, and drive 0.63% incremental Shops Ads GMV
Project objective
To help sellers become aware and adopt email opt-in with an incentive within SMA. This will help sellers to collect more customer email addresses so they can re-engage with them and drive LTV.
Goal
Business Goal: Drive adoption of 1-click email capture SFI to drive promotional parity and protect Shops Ads performance as we reduce reliance on MFI (H1 goal: drive adoption with 1700 TAM sellers which represent 20% of Shops ads revenue, H2 goal is to increase adoption among sellers which represent 50% of Shops Ads revenue)
Research Goals: Understand key pathways for adoption, which surfaces should we prioritize to drive adoption? (SMA, email, Ads Manager, etc) Ensure that end to end flow is frictionless and sets clear expectations with sellers so they know what they're opting in to Understand which unmet needs are most important to sellers to help us with prioritization
