02 Research goals and Objective
Research Goal
Maximize SFI email capture adoption |
The objective of this research is to collect seller feedback on the design, content, and functionality of the SFI email capture feature to discover potential areas of improvement to maximize seller adoption of the feature on launch.
Research Obectives
Ease of Use |
Identify any usability issues that might block or hinder seller adoption. In particular, we are interesting in knowing if: . Sellers can discover SFI email opt in Sellers can change the discount amount O Prior to enabling O After enabling Sellers can enable pre-select checkout opt-in
Understand |
Determine how well sellers interpret and comprehend how the new feature works and its impact. In particular, we are interested in how: • Sellers understand the language used in the SFI feature • Sellers understand how the feature works and what it impacts • Sellers understand all the functionality options available in the feature . Sellers can differentiate where discount will and will not be applied (e.g. check out vs. storefront/pdp)
Satisfaction |
Understand how well the SFI email opt in feature meets the sellers' needs, in particular: Does the feature meet the sellers' needs to capture email Is there any content/functionality that is missing from the feature • Seller would be confident using the feature . Perspective/thoughts/concerns about the feature
