04 Next Steps
Rethink 1-click option
All participants understood the feature, however, sellers expected to be able to configure and customize the way email collection would appear on their shop surfaces. Therefore, to facilitate Seller adoption, it may be more beneficial to create a multistep enablement process that gives sellers control and feedback on discount amount, location, language, and design/branding.
Alpha Launch Survey
The survey that is part of the Alpha Launch of the survey and includes a question about how well the feature meets sellers needs. If this result is also low, it provides more evidence that sellers want more control and/or feedback on how the feature will be enabled on their shop.
