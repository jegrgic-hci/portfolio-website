02 Research goals and Objective
Research and data collection goals
Shopify rolling research program |
Behavioral metrics provide a valuable insight into the performance of a feature, but only answers ‘what’ users are doing. Compared to predefined success metrics, this may be sufficient data collection.
However, for a new feature, without existing benchmark metrics to judge success, it is important to perform some qualitative research to understand ‘why’ users are behaving in a particular way.
Both forms of data collection and research help develop a fuller and detailed understanding of how users interact with the feature and how it can be improved to meet their needs.
